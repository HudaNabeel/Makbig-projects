Dr.APJ.Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam (1931-2015) was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India (2002-2007). Known as the "Missile Man of India" for his significant role in India's missile and nuclear programs, he worked with ISRO and DRDO, contributing to the development of the Agni and Prithvi missiles. A visionary leader and inspirational teacher, he received India's highest civilian honor, the Bharat Ratna. Dr. Kalam was deeply committed to youth empowerment, education, and national development. His humility, integrity, and dedication continue to inspire millions.
